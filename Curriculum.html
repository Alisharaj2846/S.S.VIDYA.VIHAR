Course Curriculum

The school follows the 10+2 pattern of education of the CBSE Board, preparing students for the All India Secondary School Examination of Class X and the All India Senior School Certificates Examination of Class XII.
The academic session runs from April to March.

Academics

Our major strength is a team of highly qualified, experienced trained and dedicated teaching staff added with a very co-operative non-teaching staff. Modern methodology of teaching is in practice. Teachers aim at exploring the best of students and students learning the most.

Special classes are arranged on the demand of the students.

Medium of Instructions

Medium of instructions is English but adequate emphasis is on the mother lanugage tounge.

Admission

✔ Before seeking admission for his/her wards in the school, guardian is expected to go through the various rules thoroughly given in the school prospectus.

✔ For seeking admission to the lowest class (Nursery), the child must attain the age between 31/2 to 41/2 years.

✔ Admission will be made strictly on the basis of merit.

✔ For admission to NURSERY and K.G. classes, oral discussion with the parents is imperative.

✔ Birth Certifiacte, Transfer Certifiacte (in any) and three passport size photograps are to be submitted during admission.

✔ Admission to classes (I to IX) will be made on the basis of written test and interview of the applicants.

✔ Selected candidate names will be put on the school Notice Board.

✔ Fee have to be deposited within 3 days after slection. Failing which, the seat(s) will be allotted to the candidate(s) on the waiting list.
